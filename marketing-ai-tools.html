Strategy
Campaigns launch blind - no signal on which accounts are ready to buy
Predictive Intent Engine
  • Pull topic-level surge scores from Bombora (which companies are researching your category) and G2 Buyer Intent (who's reading your reviews and comparing you to competitors)
  • Cross-reference intent signals against ICP firmographics from Clearbit or ZoomInfo (industry, headcount, revenue, tech stack)
  • Claude ranks accounts by combined intent + ICP fit score - campaigns start targeted, not broadcast

Research
Competitive monitoring is manual, reactive, and always late
Weekly Competitive Digest
  • Zapier triggers a weekly Perplexity search for each competitor (news, product launches, pricing changes, "[competitor] hiring site:linkedin.com")
  • Perplexity handles the web search layer since LinkedIn is publicly indexed by Google even though direct scraping is blocked
  • Search results are piped to Claude, which interprets hiring patterns, identifies strategic signals, and writes a ranked digest
  • Delivered to Slack every Monday - Perplexity finds, Claude thinks

Analytics
Performance data never feeds back to improve the next campaign
Closed-Loop Performance Digest
  • Zapier pulls weekly CSV exports from GA4 (via Data API), HubSpot, and Google/Meta Ads into a single Google Sheet
  • A second Zap sends the consolidated data to Claude API
  • Claude writes a plain-English summary: what moved, why, and one recommended action
  • Insights auto-update the Strategy prompt library so the next brief is smarter

Governance
AI content ships without legal, brand, or data safety checks
HITL Checkpoint Layer
  • Every AI output above a defined risk threshold (public copy, customer data, regulated claims) routes to a mandatory Notion review queue
  • Slack approval flow notifies the reviewer and tracks turnaround time
  • A separate PII-scrubbing prompt strips identifiable data from CRM exports before they touch any external LLM
⚠ HITL required before external publish
